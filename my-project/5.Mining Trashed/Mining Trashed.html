Mining Trashed

"Trashed" is decentralized as production waste and consumption waste in the world.

"Mining Trashed" – everyone in the world has the right to extract waste production and waste consumption to maintain the network "Trashed", making the world a better place.

In the world there is a practice of collecting "Trashed" such as paper, cardboard, plastics, glass, metals. There is even a practice of collecting milk, dairy farms.
